About Me

I am currently Ph.D. student in Computer Vision Lab, Dept. of Computer Science at HKU, advised by Dr. Kenneth K.Y. Wong. Prior to studying at HKU, I received my B.Eng. from Huazhong University of Science and Technology.

Research Interests

My interests are centered around Computer Vision and Deep Learning. Current research topics include face sketch synthesis and image to image translation.
